This is a private project by a group of vintage Serge enthusiasts, and circuit details, boards etc. will not be available to the general public.

Markings used for connections to pots (just to be confusing):
b = beginning or CCW end of the pot
c = center pin or WIPER of the pot
e = end or CW end of the pot

Some boards use symbols instead.

A lot of the 1973 boards were wired so that the MAX setting of pots was at the CCW end, contrary to common practice. These boards maintain that practice. Simply swap the outer wires if you require it to work the other way around.

With the exception of 1N4148 diodes, the original component values have been specified on the overlays. This in no way means that these parts are recommended. Feel free to substitute a better part where appropriate. The diodes were originally FD6666.
